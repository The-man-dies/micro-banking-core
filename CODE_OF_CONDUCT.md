# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accepting feedback professionally
- Focusing on what is best for the project
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and it also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.
